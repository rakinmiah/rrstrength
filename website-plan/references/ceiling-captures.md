## Ceiling references (craft bar)

### 1. Linear (linear.app) — fetched live
- Dark charcoal/near-black canvas, high-contrast white type, single bright accent. Geometric sans, hierarchy via **weight** not just size.
- Hero: centred short headline + sub + CTA, single asymmetric product shot below the fold line. No hero motion.
- Rhythm: breathable hero → dense product showcase → alternating asymmetric feature sections → compact testimonial grid.
- Motion: highly restrained — fade/slide scroll reveals only.
- **Memorable:** confident spareness; "no noise" authority.
- **Transferable:** (a) asymmetric section pairing, alternating direction; (b) dark canvas + 2–3 accents max + big confident headlines that breathe.

### 2. Stripe (stripe.com) — fetched live
- Typography-led; subtle fluid gradient behind hero, visual weight on words.
- Rhythm proven across long scroll: breathable hero → dense stat grid → breathable accordion case-study → moderate 3-col product grid.
- Composition: full-bleed imagery, asymmetric offset for stats/testimonials, layered case-study cards.
- Motion: restrained, user-controlled, scroll fade-ins.
- **Memorable:** massive scale communicated through restraint.
- **Transferable:** (a) alternate dense stat callouts with breathable testimonial/story cards; (b) asymmetric coach-spotlight (image one side, credentials the other), flip per section.

### 3. Nike (nike.com) — fetch blocked (403); documented from established design knowledge
- Bold **condensed uppercase** display type (Futura/Trade Gothic lineage), black/white with a single hot accent (volt).
- Full-bleed athletic photography, motion frozen at peak exertion, edge-to-edge.
- Composition: full-bleed hero with offset lower-left copy; grid of category tiles; minimal chrome.
- **Transferable:** full-bleed action photography + lower-left offset headline is the canonical athletic hero — directly applicable to RR's Ramadan-Rumble deadlift shots and bold-uppercase brand.

### 4. Apple (apple.com) — documented from established design knowledge
- Centred-focal product moments, enormous whitespace, scroll-pinned reveals, restrained palette.
- **Transferable:** the centred-focal single-statement module as a "breath" between dense sections; generous vertical rhythm signals premium.

### 5. Arc (arc.net) — documented from established design knowledge
- Crafted scroll-linked storytelling, playful but controlled motion, warm dark gradients.
- **Transferable:** one crafted scroll-linked moment (the SIGNATURE module) elevates an otherwise quiet page; everything else stays calm.

## Adjacent-category references

### Gymshark (uk.gymshark.com) — fetched live
- Deep blacks/neutrals + seasonal accent; high-energy saturated photography, mid-movement subjects.
- Composition beyond text+image: asymmetric card grids, full-bleed campaign sections, overlapping carousel cards.
- **Transferable:** neutral/dark base with a disciplined accent; energetic mid-lift crops over static posed shots.
